About Our School

Building Future Leaders Since 1990

Our History

Founded in 1990, Himchuli School has been at the forefront of educational excellence in Bhaktapur. Starting with just 50 students, we've grown into a premier educational institution serving over 1000 students annually. Our journey reflects our commitment to quality education and continuous improvement.

Named after the majestic Himchuli peak, our school stands as a symbol of high aspirations and achievement. We've consistently maintained high academic standards while nurturing students' overall development.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual curiosity, creativity, and moral values while preserving our rich Nepali cultural heritage. We aim to create global citizens rooted in local values.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nepal that shapes responsible global citizens and future leaders while maintaining excellence in both academic and extracurricular activities.
